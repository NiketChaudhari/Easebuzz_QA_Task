Question :
You are leading a team of 4 QA engineers.
The sprint velocity is dropping due to flaky automation tests.
How will you handle this situation?


Answer :
I will first analyze failure trends and identify common causes like timing issues, unstable locators, and environment inconsistencies.
I will quarantine flaky tests temporarily so they will not block the CI pipeline.
I will also arrange code reviews and standards for automation scripts to improve quality and assign each tester ownership of a failing test cluster to investigate and fix.

Question :
During production release, you find 2 critical bugs reported by the client that were missed in QA.
What’s your approach to fix gaps in process and testing?


Answer :
I will work with the development team to triage and release hotfixes quickly, while keeping stakeholders informed with clear updates.
Will conduct a blameless root cause analysis, which revealed gaps in edge-case coverage and unclear acceptance criteria.
Will also find the gap by : 
    Where did these bugs originate?
    What test cases should have caught them?
    Did they appear in regression, exploratory, or edge-case scenarios?
Will check if prod behavior differs from QA environment to investigate the Environment gap.
Will add the missed scenarios to the suite immediately.
For preventing same issue in future, I will add exploratory testing sessions before major releases, specifically targeting integration points and edge cases.
